💡 Tips for Japan
💳 IC Card
Buy Suica or Pasmo at Tokyo Station. Works on all trains, buses & konbini.
💴 Cash
¥100k–150k. Best ATMs: 7-Eleven & Japan Post at Narita.
🔌 Power
Japan uses Type A (same as Canada!) — no adapter needed.
📱 Apps
Download Google Maps & Translate offline before landing.
👟 Shoes
Wear easy slip-ons — you'll remove them constantly at temples.
🌸 Golden Week
Apr 29–May 5 is peak season. Arrive early at all major sights.
